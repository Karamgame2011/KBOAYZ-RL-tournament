Rocket League Family Tournament

Tournament Rules

5-6 players rotate in 2v2 matches. Points from Rocket League scoreboard are used to rank players. Top 4 players go to the final 2v2 match. Beginner bonuses: Zaid +50 points, Joud +30 points.

Power Ticket

The Man of the Match gets 1 Power Ticket per game. Options:

  1. Steal 150 points from another player
  2. 1.5× multiplier for next game
  3. Give a 15 sec penalty to another player

Only the tournament admin can apply tickets and edit points.
