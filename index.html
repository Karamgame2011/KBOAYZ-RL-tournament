Welcome to the Family RL Tournament!

This is a fun tournament for our family. 6 players rotate in 2v2 matches. Points from Rocket League scoreboard are used to rank players. Top 4 players go to the finals, top 1 will have the top 4 as his team-mate, top 2 will have top 3 as their team-mate, Have fun!.

Tournament Guides

  • Points from RL matches are entered by the admin.
  • Man of the Match gets a Power Ticket to use for bonuses.
  • MVP stars are awarded for Man of the Match.
